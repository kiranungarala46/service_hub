About Us

Built for Andhra Pradesh, by People Who Live Here

ServiceHub was founded in 2022 in Vijayawada with a simple belief: finding a trustworthy local repair expert should be as easy as ordering food online.

We partner with skilled tradespeople across 18 cities — verifying every provider, training them on customer service, and giving them tools to grow their business while giving customers peace of mind.

Today, we're the most trusted home services platform in Andhra Pradesh with over 2,400 providers and 50,000 completed jobs.

Trust First

Background checks, ID verification, and skill tests for every provider on our platform.

Community Driven

We support local craftspeople and help them earn better by connecting them to more customers.

Always Improving

Every booking, review, and feedback loop makes our platform smarter and our service better.
